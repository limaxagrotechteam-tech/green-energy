Our Story

15+ Years Bridging Capital & Sustainable Infrastructure

Limaxagrotech was founded to make impactful investing accessible. We meticulously select and structure investment opportunities in solar farms, wind parks, hydropower stations, and regenerative agriculture projects. Our mission is to deliver stable returns for our investors while accelerating the global transition to clean energy and sustainable food systems.

UK Regulated & Accredited

Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) – ensuring transparent, fair, and secure investment practices.
